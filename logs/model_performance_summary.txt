   Parameters: 2.4M (fewest!)
   Training Speed: Fast (fastest!)
   Use Case: Best accuracy, industry standard

   - Highest accuracy (98.10%)
   - Smallest model size (11 MB vs 400+ MB)
   - Fastest training
   - Uses pre-trained knowledge from ImageNet
   - Industry standard approach
   
   Why it works best:
   - MobileNetV2 was pre-trained on 1.4 million images
   - Already knows general image features (edges, shapes, patterns)
   - Only needs to learn "dog vs cat" specific features
   - Efficient architecture designed for mobile devices
